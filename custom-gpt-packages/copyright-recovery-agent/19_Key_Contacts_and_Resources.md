# Key Contacts and Resources

## Overview

This document provides a directory of essential tools, organizations, and databases required for executing catalog recovery and administration.

## Key Concepts

The agent leverages these external resources to verify data, draft documents, and recommend next steps for the human principal.

## Detailed Information

### Essential Databases
*   **US Copyright Office Public Records System:** `https://publicrecords.copyright.gov/` (For post-1978 registrations and all recordations).
*   **Songview (ASCAP/BMI):** `https://www.bmi.com/songview` (For performance rights splits and current publishers).
*   **The MLC Public Search:** `https://portal.themlc.com/search` (For mechanical rights administration).

### Advocacy and Tooling
*   **Authors Alliance:** `https://www.authorsalliance.org/` (Provides the Rightsback.org termination tool and free, legally vetted Notice of Termination templates).
*   **Songwriters of North America (SONA):** Advocacy group providing education on termination rights.
*   **Future of Music Coalition:** Resources on creator rights and fair compensation.

### Valuation and Trade Press
*   **Music Business Worldwide (MBW):** For tracking current catalog acquisition deals and multiples.
*   **Billboard Pro / Chartlex:** For detailed analysis of valuation metrics (NPS multiples, DCF rates).

### Administration Partners (Post-Recovery)
*   **Kobalt Music Publishing:** Leading independent administrator offering transparent, tech-driven royalty collection without taking copyright ownership.
*   **Songtrust:** Accessible publishing administration for independent and recovering songwriters.

## Practical Applications

When generating a strategic plan, the agent will provide direct links to the Authors Alliance templates for the client to review, and recommend registering the recovered works with Songtrust or Kobalt for immediate post-termination administration.

## Escalation Requirements

None directly applicable to this reference file, though the agent will escalate to human counsel before finalizing any binding agreements with the listed administration partners.
