# Catalog Valuation Methodology & Multiples

## Overview

This document explains the financial metrics used to value music catalogs, focusing on Net Publisher's Share (NPS) and market multiples. Accurate valuation is crucial for advising clients on whether to sell, hold, or activate their recovered rights.

## Key Concepts

Music catalogs are valued as income-producing assets, typically priced as a multiple of their historical recurring earnings. The multiple fluctuates based on interest rates, catalog age, genre, and perceived risk.

## Detailed Information

### Net Publisher's Share (NPS)
NPS is the gross income collected by the publisher minus the royalties paid out to the songwriters. It represents the actual cash flow available to the catalog owner. For a songwriter buying back their publishing, they are acquiring this NPS stream.

### Market Multiples (2026 Baseline)
Valuations are expressed as a multiple of the average annual NPS (usually calculated over the last 3-5 years).
*   **Independent/Standard Catalogs:** 8x to 14x NPS.
*   **Proven Earners (5+ years stable history):** 14x to 18x NPS.
*   **Blue-Chip Legacy (Springsteen, Dylan tier):** 18x to 24x NPS.

*Note: Multiples in 2026 are structurally lower than the 2021 peak (which saw 20x+ routinely) due to higher interest rates and the perceived risk of AI-generated music diluting streaming market share. A typical 2026 indie deal closes at a 12% to 16% discount rate on a DCF (Discounted Cash Flow) basis.*

### Factors Influencing the Multiple
*   **Upward Drivers:** Strong sync placement history, earnings stability (evergreen status), fame of the songwriter, healthy mix of frontline and back-catalog.
*   **Downward Drivers:** High concentration of earnings in a single hit, declining streaming numbers, controversial artists, unclear chain of title, missing split sheets.

## Practical Applications

If an heir provides royalty statements showing an average annual NPS of $80,000 for a stable, 1990s R&B catalog, the agent will model the baseline value at $800,000 (10x multiple) to $1.12M (14x multiple). The agent will then model how brand activation could increase the annual NPS, thereby exponentially increasing the total asset value.

## Escalation Requirements

Escalate to a forensic accountant or financial advisor if the provided royalty statements are incomplete, show sudden unexplained drops in revenue, or if the catalog includes complex advances and unrecouped balances.
