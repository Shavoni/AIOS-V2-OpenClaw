# Strategic Vision: Compound Value & Productization

## Overview

This document outlines the overarching strategic vision driving the agent's deployment. The agent is not merely a diagnostic tool; it is the engine for a productized service line designed to capture value at multiple tiers of the music rights ecosystem.

## Key Concepts

The core thesis is that the combination of copyright termination, foreign reversion, and new revenue streams (like AI training data licensing) has created an unprecedented window of opportunity. The strategy moves from self-serve education to done-for-you execution, and ultimately to catalog acquisition.

## Detailed Information

### The "Why Now" Imperative

Three time-sensitive realities justify this vision:
1.  **The 35-Year Window:** A massive cohort of post-1978 grants (specifically from the peak commercial era of 1989–1995) is currently hitting their 35-year termination windows under § 203. Many will lapse unexercised due to ignorance.
2.  **Accelerating Foreign Reversions:** Jurisdictions like the EU (under the DSM Directive Article 22) and Canada (Section 14(1)) are implementing or enforcing non-exploitation revocation and posthumous reversion rights.
3.  **New Revenue Categories:** AI training-data licensing is creating a new revenue category that didn't exist when original grants were signed, making recapture economically more valuable than ever.

### Productization Roadmap

The agent powers a four-tier business model:
*   **Tier 1:** Self-serve GPT subscription for songwriters/heirs (educational + diagnostic).
*   **Tier 2:** Done-with-you forensic audit + Notice preparation (productized service).
*   **Tier 3:** Done-for-you full recovery + activation engagement (high-touch, equity or revenue-share model).
*   **Tier 4:** Catalog acquisition vehicle—deploying capital to acquire undervalued, pre-recovery catalogs, then executing the recovery and activation playbook.

## Practical Applications

The agent uses this vision to guide its recommendations. If it identifies a highly valuable catalog held by heirs who prefer a cash-out, it will flag this as a "Tier 4 Acquisition Opportunity," modeling the arbitrage between the pre-recovery purchase price and the post-recovery activated value.

## Escalation Requirements

Escalation is required when moving a client from Tier 2 (audit) to Tier 3 (execution) or Tier 4 (acquisition), as these involve complex financial agreements, equity transfers, and formal legal representation that must be handled by the human principal.
