# New Revenue Streams: AI Licensing & Sync

## Overview

This document covers emerging and high-growth revenue streams that make copyright recovery more valuable today than in previous decades. It focuses on AI training data licensing and the booming synchronization market.

## Key Concepts

The economic rationale for terminating a grant is heavily bolstered by revenue streams that did not exist when the original contracts were signed in the 1970s, 80s, or 90s.

## Detailed Information

### AI Training Data Licensing
*   **The Landscape:** Generative AI models require massive amounts of high-quality training data. Music rights holders are currently establishing frameworks to license their catalogs to AI companies (e.g., OpenAI, Anthropic, Suno) for training purposes.
*   **The Value:** This represents an entirely new, potentially massive revenue category. A publisher holding a 1980s grant is currently capturing this value. By terminating the grant, the songwriter/heir recaptures the right to negotiate these AI licenses directly or through a new administrator.
*   **Re-recording Strategy (Taylor's Version):** If master rights cannot be recovered (due to WFH clauses), artists can re-record their catalogs. Owning the publishing allows them to license the *new* masters for sync and AI training, bypassing the original label.

### The Synchronization (Sync) Boom
*   **The Landscape:** The demand for music in film, television, video games, and social media (TikTok, Instagram Reels) has exploded. Sync licensing is a multi-billion dollar industry.
*   **Strategic Activation:** Sync is not just revenue; it is marketing. A major sync placement (like Kate Bush's 'Running Up That Hill' in *Stranger Things*) can drive exponential increases in streaming royalties and cultural relevance.
*   **Post-Recovery Advantage:** Legacy publishers often passively manage older catalogs. Recovering rights allows an estate to partner with an aggressive, boutique sync agency to actively pitch the catalog to music supervisors.

## Practical Applications

When presenting a recovery plan, the agent will explicitly list 'AI Training Licensing' and 'Active Sync Pitching' as immediate post-recovery revenue drivers, modeling how these streams can replace and exceed the passive income currently generated by the legacy publisher.

## Escalation Requirements

Escalate to specialized licensing counsel if an AI company approaches an estate directly for a training data license, as the terms, opt-out clauses, and revenue-sharing models for AI are currently highly volatile and legally complex.
